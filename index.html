Hermes Innovation

Hermes is best known for his capacity as a messenger
and his association with the economic and mercantile.
The spheres around him represent the various worlds
he connects, while the hoop symbolizes the infinite
cycle of perfection and innovation.

Athena Leadership

Athena stands out as a warrior and strategist.Her
golden helmet represents the value of her
knowledge.The colors that decorate her relect
her challenging and inspiring personality when
she is at the front in the battlefield.

Apollo Insight

The variety of colors represents insights and the
sensitivity with which they are percelved. They are
within Apollo due to their capacity for catharsis,
freeing empathy, creativity and intelligence.
